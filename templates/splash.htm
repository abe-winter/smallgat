How do I get started?

Ask your school or office to create an institution page.

While you're at it, create a personal account.

When you get the institution link, first make sure that it's from a trusted source, then attach yourself to the institution and wait to get placed.


Who pays for this?

For now, the author is self-funding.

If this grows and gets expensive, we'll add a $1 fee to create an institution, which will be used to cover hosting expenses and to create a paper trail for institutions. The excess will be donated to virus research or treatment.

If we add a fee, we'll publish all income, costs and donations on this site.
