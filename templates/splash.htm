Large gatherings banned in your city? Sent home from school? Can't go to work?

No sweat. Small gatherings finds small groups of people from your class or team who live nearby, then connects you up.

This product makes no claims about your risk of getting or spreading a disease, and hasn't been reviewed by a medical professional.

If you're a public official and you want to block this service in your municipality, or shrink the meeting size, we'll work with you. Please open a github issue.


How do I get started?

Ask your school or office to create an institution page, and then visit the link.

While you're at it, create a personal account. It's free and all you need is an email address.

When you get the institution link, first make sure that it's from a trusted source, then attach yourself to the institution and wait to get placed.


Who pays for this?

For now, Small gatherings is self-funded.

If this grows and gets expensive, we'll add a $1 fee to create an institution, which will be used to cover hosting expenses and to create a paper trail for institutions. The excess will be donated to virus research or treatment.

If we add a fee, we'll publish all income, costs and donations on this site.
